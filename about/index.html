The Goal

The goal of this tool to help new and intermediate players make wise character choices that help them pick a well-balanced team. I hope to ultimately improve the quality of pub play. My goal is to keep the metrics used to only quantifiable/semi-quantifiable data, and to keep the tool as simple and as easy to use as possible.

If you have any feedback or want to get involved, shoot me an email at [ellisd23] at [gmail] [dot] [com].
